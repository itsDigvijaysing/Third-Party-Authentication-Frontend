# Third-Party-Authentication-Frontend

We are excited to introduce our advanced secured third-party authentication system, designed to offer both convenience and top-notch security. Our system is built on face recognition technology, ensuring a seamless and efficient login experience for users.

In addition to face recognition, we understand the importance of adding extra layers of security. That's why we have included the option for users to enable OTP (One-Time Password) verification. This additional step adds an extra level of security, giving users peace of mind knowing their accounts are protected.

With our system, users can enjoy the convenience of quick and secure access to their accounts, all while ensuring their information remains safe and protected. Experience the future of authentication with our secured third-party authentication system.
